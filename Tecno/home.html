SHIRTS

What brands do you print on?

All Ugmonk t-shirts are primted on American Apparel and are 100% manufactured and printed in the USA.


How do I know what size shirt to order?

Each product page has links to sizing charts. Refer to these charts and any special instructions given per product.


I ordered two shirts of the same size, why is one smaller?

Some shirts are prewashed because of the printing technique used. These shirts may be slightly smaller when they arrive. Also, a few designs are printed on different style shirts. Check the product descrip- tion for any special sizing notes.


Will you notify me when you release a new design or restock a current design?

Of course. Subscribe to the Ugmonk newsletter, follow us on twitter, or keep tabs on our blog. We post updates about our products as well as occasional sales and specials.


Do you accept designs from third parties?

No, all of our designs are Ugmonk originals, designed by Ugmonk's founder Jeff Sheldon.
